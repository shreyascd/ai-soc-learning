# Day 7 – Reflection

1. **Faster daily reporting** – reusing a summarize/explain template for incident notes means not rebuilding the same structure by hand every time, cutting down on repetitive writing.
2. **More consistent alert triage** – a fixed classify template with the same category list keeps labeling consistent across days, instead of wording drifting depending on mood or time pressure.
3. **Easier handoffs** – templated prompts (and their outputs) are easier for teammates to read and reuse, since the structure is predictable instead of a one-off improvised question each time.
