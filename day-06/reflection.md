# Day 6 – Reflection

Asking the model to "think step by step" improves reasoning because it forces the model to generate intermediate logic before landing on a conclusion, instead of jumping straight to a pattern-matched guess. This slows down the generation process in a useful way — each step builds on the last, so mistakes are less likely to compound silently. It also makes the reasoning visible, so as the human reviewer I can spot exactly where the logic went wrong if the final answer is off, rather than just getting a black-box conclusion I have to trust blindly.
